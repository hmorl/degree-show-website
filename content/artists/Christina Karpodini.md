Christina Karpodini is a Greek composer, media artist, and singer, currently living in London. Her compositional practice focuses on electroacoustic compositions with synthesized sounds, pre-recorded sounds, and instruments. She is currently exploring interactive computational forms that would enable a better perception of electroacoustic music. Her musical education started at an early age with classical piano training and participation in vocal ensembles. She has graduated with the Diploma in Music Harmony from the conservatory in 2011 and with the Diploma in Counterpoint Music in 2013, both in Athens. In 2015, she obtained the Piano Teaching Diploma (equivalent to LRAM) and the Bachelor Degree in Music Studies, pathway Music Technology and Acoustics, with first-class Honors by the National University of Athens.  In 2016, she completed the Master Degree in Composition at City University of London. She is currently undertaking the second Master in Computational Arts at Goldsmiths.
